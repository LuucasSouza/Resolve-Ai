# ADR-0020 - Adopt Weighted Decision Matrix

## Status

Accepted

## Context

AI-SEOS needs a consistent way to compare meaningful alternatives.

## Decision

Adopt weighted decision matrices as the standard decision comparison method.

## Rationale

Weights make priorities explicit while preserving human judgment.

## Consequences

Decision reasoning becomes clearer. Scores may be misused if treated as objective truth.

## Trade-offs

Gains transparency. Requires rationale for weights and scores.
