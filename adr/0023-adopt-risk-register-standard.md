# ADR-0023 - Adopt Risk Register Standard

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Active project risks need a canonical control surface.

## Decision

Adopt Risk Register Standard and risk assessment templates.

## Rationale

Risks need ownership, status, scoring, review cadence and links to decisions.

## Consequences

Risk tracking becomes actionable and auditable.

## Trade-offs

Gains visibility. Requires regular updates.
