# ADR-0025 - Adopt Optimization Engine

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Decision and risk review should be followed by structured simplification and improvement before execution.

## Decision

Adopt Optimization Engine as the operating engine for simplicity, cost, complexity, maintainability, scalability, security, reversibility and AI usage review.

## Rationale

Execution should not convert avoidable complexity into backlog.

## Consequences

Projects get an explicit improvement loop before execution.

## Trade-offs

Gains better plans. Adds another review stage.
