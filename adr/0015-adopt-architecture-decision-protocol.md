# ADR-0015 - Adopt Architecture Decision Protocol

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Architecture decisions cannot wait for the full Decision Engine planned in Sprint 3.

## Problem

Significant architecture choices need a protocol now for framing decisions, comparing options, assessing reversibility and producing ADRs.

## Decision

Adopt Architecture Decision Protocol as the Sprint 2 decision workflow for architecture choices.

## Options Considered

- Defer all decision process to Sprint 3.
- Use informal ADR creation only.
- Create an interim architecture decision protocol.

## Consequences

Architecture decisions can be made with discipline immediately. Some overlap with future Decision Engine is expected.

## Trade-offs

Gains: immediate decision quality. Costs: future consolidation with Decision Engine may be needed.

## Reversibility

High. Sprint 3 may supersede or generalize this protocol.

## Follow-up

Integrate with Decision Engine and extended ADR lifecycle in Sprint 3.
