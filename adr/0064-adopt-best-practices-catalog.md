# ADR 0064 - Adopt Best Practices Catalog

## Status

Accepted

## Version

0.7.0

## Date

2026-07-04

## Context

Sprint 7 consolidates the initial AI-SEOS construction cycle into public-facing documentation, protocols, cases, quality audits and community onboarding.

## Decision

AI-SEOS documents repeatable practices mapped to engines, entry modes and templates.

## Consequences

The repository becomes easier to adopt, review, extend and validate, but future work must prioritize real usage and automation instead of adding more conceptual modules.

## Responsibilities

Maintainers must keep related docs, registries, templates, examples and changelog entries synchronized.
