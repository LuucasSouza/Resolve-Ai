# ADR 0245: Keep Guided Interview Local and Non-Telemetric

## Status

Accepted

## Date

2026-07-06

## Context

The interview may capture product intent and high-level constraints, so privacy rules must remain strict.

## Decision

Keep the guided interview local, non-telemetric, and free from external API calls or automatic feedback submission.

## Consequences

Participant trust is preserved. Feedback must still be shared manually.

## Alternatives Considered

- Send interview data automatically: rejected due privacy and alpha safety policy.

## Relation to Real Feedback

All private-alpha feedback emphasized high safety perception as a key product value.
