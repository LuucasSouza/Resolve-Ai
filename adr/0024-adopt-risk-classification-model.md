# ADR-0024 - Adopt Risk Classification Model

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Security, compliance, vendor and AI risks need explicit treatment.

## Decision

Adopt AI-SEOS risk taxonomy with specialized models for security, compliance, vendor and AI risk.

## Rationale

These risks can be severe even when product functionality appears correct.

## Consequences

Risk review becomes more precise. Specialized checklists must be maintained.

## Trade-offs

Gains early detection. Adds category-specific work.
