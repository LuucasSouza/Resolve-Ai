# ADR-0022 - Adopt Risk Engine

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Decision and architecture outputs need structured risk review before optimization and execution.

## Decision

Adopt Risk Engine as the operating engine for risk identification, scoring, response and monitoring.

## Rationale

Risk that is not documented is not controlled.

## Consequences

Risks receive owners, levels and response strategies. High risks require explicit mitigation or acceptance.

## Trade-offs

Gains operational control. Adds review overhead.
