# ADR-0026 - Adopt Cost Complexity Scalability Optimization

## Status

Accepted

## Context

AI-SEOS needs concrete optimization models for cost, complexity and scalability.

## Decision

Adopt Cost, Complexity and Scalability Optimization models, including AI cost optimization.

## Rationale

These models help avoid underengineering and overengineering.

## Consequences

Optimization becomes more concrete and measurable.

## Trade-offs

Gains practical review models. Requires maintaining cost and scale assumptions.
