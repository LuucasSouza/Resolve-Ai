# ADR-0021 - Adopt ADR Decision Lifecycle

## Status

Accepted

## Context

ADRs must support creation, update, superseding and decision logs without rewriting history.

## Decision

Adopt ADR Decision Protocol and ADR levels ADR-L0 through ADR-L5.

## Rationale

Different decision sizes need different documentation weight.

## Consequences

ADR governance becomes clearer. Maintainers must preserve historical reasoning.

## Trade-offs

Gains traceability. Adds lifecycle discipline.
