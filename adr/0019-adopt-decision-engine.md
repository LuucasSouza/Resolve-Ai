# ADR-0019 - Adopt Decision Engine

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Sprint 3 needs a formal engine to turn product and architecture candidates into explicit decisions.

## Decision

Adopt Decision Engine as the operating engine for significant engineering decisions.

## Rationale

AI-assisted engineering increases speed but can hide assumptions and trade-offs.

## Consequences

Decisions become traceable and reviewable. More structured review is required before execution.

## Trade-offs

Gains decision memory and governance. Adds documentation effort.
