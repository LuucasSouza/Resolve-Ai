# Protocol Review Checklist

- [ ] Purpose is clear.
- [ ] When-to-use and when-not-to-use are explicit.
- [ ] Input and output contracts are complete.
- [ ] Actors and responsibilities are named.
- [ ] Steps are executable by humans or AI agents.
- [ ] Quality gates are testable.
- [ ] Failure modes and escalation rules exist.
- [ ] Related templates and ADRs are linked.
- [ ] Protocol does not duplicate an existing protocol.
- [ ] Registry entry is updated.
