# Master Discovery Checklist

- [ ] Raw idea, requester, expected output and urgency captured.
- [ ] Problem statement, affected users, pain, frequency and current workaround documented.
- [ ] Primary users, secondary users, buyer, approver and support owner identified.
- [ ] Current alternatives, competitors, substitutes and switching costs documented.
- [ ] Business objective, value hypothesis, success metrics and decision owner documented.
- [ ] Glossary, entities, workflows, domain events and invariants started.
- [ ] Integrations, data, auth, scale, reliability, observability and AI needs captured.
- [ ] Security, compliance, data sensitivity and audit needs considered.
- [ ] Assumptions, constraints and risks registered.
- [ ] MVP goal, in scope, out of scope, deferred work and learning goals documented.
- [ ] Validation plan and handoff package created.
