# Quick Discovery Checklist - D0

- [ ] What is the idea?
- [ ] What problem does it solve?
- [ ] Who benefits?
- [ ] What is the smallest useful version?
- [ ] What assumptions are being made?
- [ ] What could go wrong?
- [ ] What is the next action?
