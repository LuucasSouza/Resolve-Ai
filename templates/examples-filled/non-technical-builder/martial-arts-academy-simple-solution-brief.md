# Martial Arts Academy Simple Solution Brief

## Problem

The academy loses time and money because student, attendance and payment information is spread across paper, spreadsheets and WhatsApp.

## Who will use it

Owner, instructors and front desk helper.

## First version should include

Student list, active/inactive status, class attendance, payment status and simple weekly dashboard.

## First version should not include

Native mobile app, automatic card billing, AI retention prediction and complex CRM automation.

## Risks in simple language

Student personal information must be protected. Payment status mistakes may create awkward conversations.

## Recommended next step

Move to Discovery Engine with a clear MVP boundary.
