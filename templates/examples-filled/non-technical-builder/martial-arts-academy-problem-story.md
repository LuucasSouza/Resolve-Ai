# Martial Arts Academy Problem Story

## Problem

The academy owner tracks students in a notebook, payments in WhatsApp messages and attendance on paper sheets. It is hard to know who missed classes, who is overdue and which students may leave soon.

## Today

New students are written down manually. Teachers mark attendance on paper. Payment reminders are sent one by one in WhatsApp.

## Pain

Information is repeated, students are forgotten, payment follow-up is delayed and the owner cannot see a clear weekly picture.

## Smallest useful improvement

Register students, mark attendance and see who is overdue.

## AI-SEOS translation

Likely solution type: simple web app.
Core users: owner, instructors, front desk.
Core information to save: students, plans, attendance, payment status.
Possible first version: student registry, class check-in and overdue list.

> AI-SEOS note: Payment automation is deferred until manual payment-state validation is reliable.
