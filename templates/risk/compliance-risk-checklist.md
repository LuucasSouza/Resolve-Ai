# Compliance Risk Checklist

- [ ] Personal data processing identified.
- [ ] Sensitive personal data identified.
- [ ] Payment, health, education or regulated data checked.
- [ ] Consent, deletion/export and retention needs considered.
- [ ] Audit logs considered.
- [ ] Data residency considered.
- [ ] AI user-impacting decisions reviewed.
