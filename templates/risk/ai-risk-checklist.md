# AI Risk Checklist

- [ ] Prompt injection assessed.
- [ ] Sensitive data sent to models reviewed.
- [ ] Tool permissions scoped.
- [ ] AI outputs validated.
- [ ] Human review defined for high-impact actions.
- [ ] Token cost monitored.
- [ ] Logs avoid sensitive prompt/output leakage.
