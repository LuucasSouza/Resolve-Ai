# Risk Taxonomy

- Product risk.
- Technical risk.
- Security risk.
- Compliance and legal risk.
- Operational risk.
- Cost risk.
- Delivery risk.
- Vendor and lock-in risk.
- AI-specific risk.

## Specialized Treatment

Security, compliance, vendor and AI risks receive explicit checklists and escalation rules.
