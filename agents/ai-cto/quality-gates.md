# Quality Gates

## Objetivo

Definir condições mínimas para considerar uma saída do AI CTO Agent completa.

## Gates

- Problem is clearly stated.
- Target users are identified.
- Buyer or value owner is identified.
- Assumptions are documented.
- Constraints are documented.
- MVP is defined.
- Architecture alternatives are considered when architecture is in scope.
- Major decisions have draft ADRs.
- Risks are documented.
- Handoff is actionable.
- Open questions are separated from decisions.

## Anti-Patterns

- Technology-first design.
- Architecture theater.
- False certainty.
- Enterprise overkill.
- Startup chaos.
- No handoff.
