# Optimization Review

SenseiHub optimization consolidates MVP simplification, cost review and scalability review.

## Decisions

- Defer native mobile app.
- Defer advanced AI retention prediction.
- Defer full payment automation until manual reconciliation is validated.
- Keep the MVP focused on students, attendance, manual payment status and dashboard.
