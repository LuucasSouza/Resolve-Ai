# Martial Arts Academy Across Three Modes

## Raw Idea

I want a simple system to control students, payments and attendance for a martial arts academy.

## Non-Technical Builder

The owner needs to stop using paper, WhatsApp and spreadsheets. First useful version: student list, attendance and overdue payments.

## Vibe Coder

Build a small web app in slices: scaffold, students, attendance, manual payment status, dashboard and deploy checklist.

## Professional Engineer

Design a modular SaaS with Student, ClassSession, AttendanceRecord, PaymentAccount, BillingStatus and RetentionRisk. ADRs required for stack, tenancy and authorization.

## Countermeasure

AI-SEOS prevents premature billing automation, prompt-and-pray implementation and hidden privacy risk.
