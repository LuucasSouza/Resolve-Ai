# Discovery Depth Levels

| Level | Use When | Output Depth |
|---|---|---|
| D0 - Quick Frame | Small idea or internal improvement | Short brief |
| D1 - Standard Discovery | New feature or small product | Full discovery document |
| D2 - Strategic Discovery | New product, major investment, high risk | Discovery + validation plan + stakeholder review |
| D3 - Enterprise Discovery | Compliance, large org, integration risk | Discovery + architecture and security pre-review |

## Quality Gate

Escolha o menor nível suficiente para risco, custo e irreversibilidade da iniciativa.
