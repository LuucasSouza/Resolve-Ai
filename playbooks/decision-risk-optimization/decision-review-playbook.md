# Decision Review Playbook

## Steps

1. Confirm decision candidate.
2. Frame decision question.
3. Generate at least three real alternatives.
4. Build weighted decision matrix.
5. Produce preliminary recommendation.
6. Send to Risk Review.

## Checklist

- [ ] Decision question is explicit.
- [ ] Decision class assigned.
- [ ] At least three alternatives included.
- [ ] Criteria and weights are explicit.
- [ ] Trade-offs documented.
- [ ] Reversibility assessed.
- [ ] ADR requirement evaluated.
