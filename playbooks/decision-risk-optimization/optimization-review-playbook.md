# Optimization Review Playbook

## Steps

1. Understand baseline.
2. Review simplicity, cost, complexity, maintainability, scalability, security, reversibility and AI usage.
3. Generate optimization options.
4. Evaluate trade-offs and new risks.
5. Recommend keep, simplify or strengthen.
6. Update dependent artifacts.

## Checklist

- [ ] Simplicity reviewed.
- [ ] Cost reviewed.
- [ ] Complexity reviewed.
- [ ] Scalability reviewed.
- [ ] Security reviewed.
- [ ] Reversibility reviewed.
- [ ] AI usage reviewed when applicable.
