# Risk Review Playbook

## Steps

1. Identify risks from recommendation.
2. Classify by taxonomy.
3. Score probability and impact.
4. Assign owner.
5. Select avoid, mitigate, transfer, accept or monitor.
6. Update risk register.

## Checklist

- [ ] Risks identified by category.
- [ ] Probability and impact scored.
- [ ] Owners assigned.
- [ ] High/critical risks mitigated or accepted.
- [ ] Accepted risks approved.
