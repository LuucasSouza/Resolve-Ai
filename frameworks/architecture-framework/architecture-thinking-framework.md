# Architecture Thinking Framework

## Core Questions

- What product outcome must the system enable?
- What quality attributes matter most?
- What constraints are real?
- What decisions are reversible?
- What must be decided now?
- What can be delayed?
- What is the simplest architecture preserving future optionality?
