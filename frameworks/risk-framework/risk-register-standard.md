# Risk Register Standard

Risk registers must be current, owned, prioritized, connected to decisions, reviewed regularly, actionable and explicit about accepted risk.

## Required Sections

- Metadata.
- Summary by level.
- Active risks.
- Critical risks.
- High risks.
- Accepted risks.
- Closed risks.
- Review notes.
