# Risk Assessment Framework

## Risk Statement

```text
If [cause/event/condition], then [impact/consequence], affecting [asset/outcome/stakeholder].
```

## Risk Score

```text
risk_score = probability * impact
```

## Levels

| Score | Level |
|---:|---|
| 1-4 | Low |
| 5-9 | Medium |
| 10-16 | High |
| 17-25 | Critical |
